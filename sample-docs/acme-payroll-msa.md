# Acme Payroll Systems Master Services Agreement

## Parties
This agreement is between ExampleCo and Acme Payroll Systems for payroll processing and employee compensation administration services.

## Services
Acme Payroll Systems will provide payroll calculation, tax filing support, direct deposit processing, and employee pay statement generation.

## Data Access
Acme Payroll Systems may access employee names, addresses, compensation details, tax identifiers, bank account information, and employment status.

## Term and Renewal
The initial term begins April 1, 2026 and ends March 31, 2027. The agreement automatically renews for one-year terms unless either party gives written notice at least 60 days before renewal.

## Fees
Annual subscription fees are $85,000. Additional implementation services may be billed separately.

## Confidentiality
Both parties agree to protect confidential information using reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards.

## Security
Acme Payroll Systems states that it uses encryption in transit and at rest. The agreement does not include a current SOC 2 report attachment.

## Breach Notification
Acme Payroll Systems will notify ExampleCo of a confirmed security incident without undue delay. No specific notification deadline is stated.

## Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach remains uncured for 30 days after written notice.

## Data Return and Deletion
Upon termination, Acme Payroll Systems will return or delete customer data upon written request. The agreement does not specify a deletion timeline.
