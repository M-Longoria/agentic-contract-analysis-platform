# CloudLedger Finance SOC 2 Summary

## Report Overview
CloudLedger Finance completed a SOC 2 Type II examination covering the period January 1, 2025 through December 31, 2025.

## Trust Services Criteria
The report covers Security, Availability, and Confidentiality.

## Scope
The examination covers the CloudLedger financial reporting platform, production infrastructure, access management, change management, and incident response processes.

## User Access
CloudLedger performs quarterly access reviews for production systems. Privileged access is restricted to approved engineering and operations personnel.

## Encryption
Customer data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher. Customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.

## Change Management
Production changes are tracked through a ticketing system and require approval before deployment.

## Incident Response
CloudLedger maintains a documented incident response plan. Security incidents are reviewed by the security team and escalated based on severity.

## Vendor Management
CloudLedger reviews critical vendors annually. Evidence of vendor risk reviews is maintained internally.

## Exceptions
The SOC 2 summary states that no high-severity control exceptions were identified during the review period.

## Notes
This summary is not a full SOC 2 report. Customers may request the full report under NDA.
