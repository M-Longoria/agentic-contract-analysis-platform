# Northstar Analytics Data Processing Addendum

## Purpose
This Data Processing Addendum applies to analytics services provided by Northstar Analytics to ExampleCo.

## Processing Activities
Northstar Analytics processes customer usage data, account identifiers, email addresses, product events, and reporting metrics.

## Roles
ExampleCo is the controller. Northstar Analytics acts as processor for personal data processed under the services agreement.

## Subprocessors
Northstar Analytics may use subprocessors for cloud hosting, logging, and data analytics support. A current subprocessor list is available upon request.

## Security Measures
Northstar Analytics maintains access controls, encryption in transit, encryption at rest, audit logging, and employee security training.

## Data Retention
Customer data is retained for the duration of the service agreement. The DPA does not specify how long data is retained after termination.

## Data Subject Requests
Northstar Analytics will reasonably assist ExampleCo with data subject access, deletion, and correction requests.

## Breach Notification
Northstar Analytics will notify ExampleCo within 72 hours after confirming a personal data breach.

## International Transfers
International transfers may occur when support or infrastructure resources are located outside the United States. Transfer mechanisms are not specified.

## Deletion
Upon written request, Northstar Analytics will delete or return personal data after contract termination.
